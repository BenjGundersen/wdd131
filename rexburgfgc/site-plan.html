Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to showcase the fighting game community here in Rexburg and make a record of the events that take place.

Audience

The audience for this site is people like myself. People who enjoy fighting games and like to document the community.

Dynamic elements

I want to have a gallery, of sorts, similar to the coolpics assignment we did, where it shows popular YouTube videos, and livestreams for our community. It will use javascript to update itself.
